Are you dreaming to study in a university, but fear being rejected? We will help to get you there!

We are an online project helping applicants from all over the world secure a spot in universities in Switzerland and the United Kingdom. Undergraduate and post-graduate programs. With and without financial aid.
Through trial and error, rejection and admission, we built knowledge and expertise which allows us to provide guidance and advice to our clients. Together, we have walked all the way through the application and admission process. We would like to offer you a helping hand and get you there!
