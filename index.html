Are you dreaming to study in a university, but fear being rejected? We will help to get you there!

We are an online project helping applicants from all over the world secure a spot in universities in Switzerland and the United Kingdom. Undergraduate and post-graduate programs. With and without financial aid.
Through trial and error, rejection and admission, we built knowledge and expertise which allows us to provide guidance and advice to our clients. Together, we have walked all the way through the application and admission process. We would like to offer you a helping hand and get you there!

Our Services

We offer the following services

  • Designing an individual strategy plan of admission to university of your choce
  • Assessing chances of admission to a university of your choice
  • Searching for study programs, scholarships and grants
  • Reviewing and correcting personal statement, letter of recommendation, CV
  • Drafting personal statement, letter of reco
  • Preparing for admission interviews
  • Preparing for IELTS/TOEFL exams

Free Initial Consultation

    Before providing any service, we conduct a free consultation during which we discuss your educational background, professional experience and future goals, explain how we can help you and answer your questions. It is important for us to have an opportunity to get to know each other better.

We cannot help with

  • Pass exams on your behalf
  • Motivate you if you are not motivated yourself
  • Prepare for IELTS/TOEFL exams within 5 days
